Welcome to SimQN's documentation!

Welcome to SimQN's documentation. SimQN is a discrete-event based network simulation platform for quantum networks. SimQN enables large-scale investigations, including QKD protocols, entanglement distributions protocols, and routing algorithms in quantum networks.
